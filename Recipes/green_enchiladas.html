G R E E N   E N C H I L A D A S   W I T H   C H I C K E N



Servings

  • 4

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kilos chicken breast
  • 1/2 onions
  • 10 arbol chiles
  • 7 tomatillos
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 sprig fresh cilantro, disinfected
  • 1 romaine lettuce, in juliana
  • 1 queso fresco
  • 16 tortillas
  • vegetable oil
  • salt
  • 1/4 cups sour cream

Steps

  1. First, cook the chicken with a piece of onion, water and salt. Since this cooked crumbles it.
  2. In a separate saucepan, cook the tomatoes and chilies for 5 minutes in boiling water.
  3. Now put in the blender, the boiled tomatoes, the chilies, the garlic, the cilantro and put it to season with salt.
  4. In a frying pan, fry the cooked meat in 2 tablespoons of hot oil until golden brown. Once it is golden, add a couple of tablespoons of the sauce to the meat.
  5. Heat a little oil in a pan, and through it, pass the tortillas on both sides, and then, pass it through the sauce. Then, refill it with the meat (1 or 2 tablespoons will be enough) and roll to shape the enchilada. Put them on the plates where you will serve the enchiladas.
